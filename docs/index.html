My work

I apply artificial intelligence driven generative design to provide solutions in science and engineering. Currently, I focus on the reinforcement learning guided design of structural and electrical elements and components, and offshore dynamics. But I am constantly expanding into the new ways that AI can be applied to STEM.

The software I develop consists of two moduli: offshore dynamics and topology optimization. It uses artificial intelligence to assist an engineer in her design. The software can be used as research or engineering analysis tool for different mechanical structures, riser systems, mooring systems, pipelay, renewables, etc.

Mission

Solving creativity to advance science and engineering. Making AI accessible.

Technological explosion, meaning an exponential growth in the rate of scientific discoveries, will happen when everyone gets access to powerful AI which only few can afford these days. At Gigala, I am on the quest to make this happen sooner.
