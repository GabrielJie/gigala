What we do

We apply artificial intelligence driven generative design to provide state-of-the-art solutions in science and engineering. Currently, we focus on the reinforcement learning guided design of structural and electrical elements and components, but we are constantly expanding into the new ways that AI can be applied to engineering.

Solving creativity to advance science and engineering. Making AI accessible.
